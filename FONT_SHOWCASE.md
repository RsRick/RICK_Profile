## üîç How to Choose

1. **Consider your brand:** Professional? Creative? Technical?
2. **Think about readability:** Body text needs high readability
3. **Create contrast:** Pair serif with sans-serif
4. **Limit variety:** Use 2-3 fonts maximum
5. **Test on devices:** Check mobile and desktop
6. **Consider language:** Use Bangla fonts for Bengali text

## üöÄ Quick Tips

- **Headings:** Use Display or Serif fonts
- **Body:** Use Sans-serif fonts for screens
- **Emphasis:** Use italic or bold, not different fonts
- **Hierarchy:** Use font size and weight, not just different fonts
- **Consistency:** Stick to your chosen fonts throughout
