## üí° How to Use

### In Font Selector
1. Open any admin form (Header, About Me, etc.)
2. Click on font selector dropdown
3. Click **"Certificate"** category filter
4. Browse 12 elegant certificate fonts
5. Select and preview in real-time

### Best Practices
- **Use for names only** - Recipient names, signatures
- **Pair with readable fonts** - Serif for body, Sans-serif for details
- **Size appropriately** - 36-48px for names, 24-32px for titles
- **Don't overuse** - Maximum 2 certificate fonts per design
